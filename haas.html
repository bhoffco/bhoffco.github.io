A Note From Us On Security

Listed below are links to resources and articles about security breaches. Education and awareness is the best sales tool for us.

If you have a business that does $1mil, $5mil, even $10mil or more per year a data breach can end the business. If fact, there are multiple studies that show an average of 60% of small businesses are out of business within 6 months of a data breach.

Having a properly structured and monitored network can be a painful investment. Especially if you are handling your own IT right now. But the cost is worth it.

Sending letters to all your prospects/customers, dealing with media and hiring expensive forensic experts is how you will have to respond to a breach.

Customers ask us "why would somebody target my company?" Hackers randomnly hit every vulnerable network they can find. Once inside a network, privileges get escaleted and sensitive data can be extracted.

An attacker can remain inside a vulnerable network for months without detection as you can see below.

Having a well structured/monitored network will give you peace of mind and distinguish you from most of your competition.
