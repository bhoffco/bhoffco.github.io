A Note From Us On Security

Thank you for checking out our site. We have decided to make this page more about information and education than describing how we protect our clients.

Listed below this note are links to resources and articles about security breaches. We are going to continue to add to this as time goes on. Education and awareness is the best sales tool for us.

We have many small and medium sized customers. These tend to be law firms, medical offices, accountants, and sales organizations.

The breach cost statistics above are correct. If you have a business that does $5mil, $10 mil, even $20mil per year a data breach can end the business. We have seen this happen.

Having a properly setup and monitored network can be expensive. Especially if you are handling your own IT right now. But the cost is worth it.

Dealing with media, sending letters to all your customers and hiring forensic experts who will charge a fortune is how you will have to respond to a breach.

Hackers generally do not target individual companies. They target anything and everything they can penetrate. Then decide what to do with the stolen data. Unfortunately, securing your network is like buying insurance. Nobody likes to do it.

Please take a look at the links and stories below and let us know if you would like to chat. It may be less expensive than you think to have a properly secured network.
