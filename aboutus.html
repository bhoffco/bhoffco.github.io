Our History

Located in the heart of Boca Raton Florida, Level5 Technology was founded in 2010 with 2 basic yet important principles in mind. Evolve with technology, and insure client satisfaction. Under the guidance of IT Director Ben Filippelli, known all over as a guru in the field of technology, Level5 Mgmt was able to create a fully redundant IT environment and keep core IT services controlled internally without having to outsource to other vendors. Matched with our signature “Above and Beyond” service Level5 Technology has stayed true to those principles and grown to be a trusted name in the IT service community.

Where Did The Name Come From?

The name Level5 derives from the popular book “Good to Great” by Jim Collins. Level 5 leadership refers to the highest level in a hierarchy of executive capabilities. After several years of research, Collins discovered that all of the great organizations he studied were headed by what he referred to as "Level 5 Leaders." These C- level Leaders have a unique combination of fierce resolve and humility. They do not seek success for their own glory; rather, success is necessary so that the team and organization can thrive.

Leaders at the other four levels may be successful, but are unable to elevate companies from mediocrity to sustained excellence. A level 5 leader is one who can sustain over a long term a company’s performance to supersede all of its competitors.
